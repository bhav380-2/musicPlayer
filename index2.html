ColdPlay

British Band

Coldplay is a british music band that has enjoyed continuous success since their inception in 1996 through their intelligent and soulful songs. The band members are Chris Martin(lead vocals), John Buckland(lead Guitar), Guy Berryman(bass guitar) and Will Champion(Drums).
